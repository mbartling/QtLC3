NOT

Bit-wise complement

Assembler Formats

NOT DR, SR

Encodings

Check Here

Operation

    
      DR = ~SR;
      setcc();

Description

The bit-wise complement of the contents of SR is stored in DR. The condition codes are set, based on whether the binary value produced, taken as a twos complement integer, is negative, zero, or positive.

Examples

    
      NOT R4, R2    ;R4 = NOT(R2)
